About Green Earth Initiative

Learn about our journey, mission, and the passionate team driving change.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Green Earth Initiative began as a small community group in Cape Town focused on local beach cleanups.
Today, we've grown into a national movement with projects spanning tree planting, wildlife conservation, environmental education, and sustainable community development.

Our Mission

To preserve and restore South Africa's natural environment through collaborative conservation efforts, education,
and empowering communities to become stewards of their local ecosystems.

Our Vision

A sustainable future where communities and nature thrive together, and environmental conservation is at the heart of all development decisions.
